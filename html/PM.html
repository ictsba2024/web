Preventive measures

Communicable diseases

Vaccination

Vaccination help prevents the spread of disease and reduces disease risk

Wear personal protective equipment

Wear personal protective equipment like marks, gloves properly help reduce the risk.

Perform hand hygiene

The general public is advised to wash hands thoroughly with soap and water when hands are visibly dirty or likely contaminated with blood and fluids from the body. When hands are not obviously soiled, they can be sanitized using an alcohol-based hand sanitizer containing 70-80% alcohol.

Store food wisely

Store the food in our school's fridge in order to reduce the risk of food poisoning.

Sick leave

Once you have got infectious disease, please take sick leave immediately.

Noncommunicable diseases

Have balanced diet

It is better for you to matain a balanced diet so as to provide enough nutrition for your growth.

See more
Be physically active

Aim for at least 30-60 minutes of moderate physical activity per day since it is great for both the body and mind.


Mantain an optimum body weight

Maintaining a normal BMI through balanced nutrition and exercise helps prevent chronic diseases while promoting bone and muscle development.

Check your BMI!
Manage stress

Too much cortisol from chronic stress will contribute to chronic diseases. Try to exercise periodically.
